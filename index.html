Welcome to the Canvas Lab, a research group at Reichman University working at the intersection of Computer Vision, Computer Graphics, and Generative AI. Our work spans generative models for art and photography, multimodal systems combining vision and language, 3D graphics for editing and gaming, and innovative tools that push the boundaries of human–machine creativity. We aim to advance both practical applications and computational approaches that empower creators and intelligent systems alike.

Latent News

  • We just launched our lab website – welcome!

    After months of development, we're excited to announce the launch of our new Canvas Lab website. This platform will serve as a hub for our research activities, publications, and team updates. The site features our latest projects in computer vision, graphics, and generative AI, and will be regularly updated with new content and discoveries.

    We've designed the site to be both informative for the research community and accessible to those interested in learning more about our work at the intersection of creativity and technology.

  • Our paper on Conditional Balance was presented at CVPR 2025!

    We're thrilled to share that our research on "Conditional Balance: A Novel Approach to Generative Model Stability" has been accepted and presented at the Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR) 2025 conference.

    This work introduces a new method for improving the stability and quality of generative models by implementing conditional balance mechanisms. The paper demonstrates significant improvements in image generation quality and model convergence rates across multiple benchmark datasets.

    The presentation was well-received by the community, sparking interesting discussions about future directions in generative AI research.

Research

Explore our cutting-edge research through visual examples of our work in computer vision, graphics, and generative AI.

Generative Art & Photography

We develop novel generative models that create stunning visual art and photography. Our research focuses on improving the quality, diversity, and controllability of AI-generated images, enabling artists to explore new creative possibilities through human-AI collaboration.

Multimodal Vision-Language Systems

Our multimodal systems bridge the gap between visual understanding and natural language processing. These tools can analyze images, generate descriptive text, and respond to complex queries that require both visual and linguistic reasoning.

3D Graphics & Interactive Editing

We create innovative 3D graphics tools for content creation, editing, and interactive experiences. Our work includes neural rendering techniques, 3D scene understanding, and real-time graphics applications for gaming and virtual environments.

Computational Photography

Our computational photography research transforms how we capture, process, and enhance images. We develop algorithms that can improve image quality, enable new photographic techniques, and create tools that extend the capabilities of traditional cameras.

Neural Rendering & 3D Content Creation

We pioneer neural rendering techniques that can generate photorealistic 3D content from various inputs. Our methods enable efficient creation of 3D models, textures, and animations, revolutionizing content production for media and entertainment.

AI-Powered Creative Tools

We design intelligent creative tools that augment human creativity rather than replace it. These systems understand artistic intent, provide intelligent suggestions, and enable new forms of creative expression through seamless human-AI collaboration.
