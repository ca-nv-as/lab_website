Welcome to Canvas Lab

Welcome to the Canvas Lab at Reichman University, where we explore the intersection of computer graphics, computer vision, and artificial intelligence. Our research focuses on creating innovative solutions for visual computing challenges, from generative AI and neural rendering to computational photography and interactive graphics systems.

We are committed to advancing the state-of-the-art in visual computing while fostering a collaborative environment where students and researchers can develop cutting-edge technologies that have real-world impact.
